### Certification FAQ

**Q: Do I need certification for freelance sworn testimony sessions?**

**Q: Can I work out of state with my certification?**
A: Sometimes. License transfer is growing, but many states still require a local exam or license.

**Q: Is realtime required?**
A: Not everywhere, but realtime capability can raise your rate and open high-end assignments.

**Q: How many times can I take the test?**
A: Most tests allow multiple attempts. Each attempt requires a fee. Check the test calendar and windows. [11]

**Q: Do courts accept digital reporters for trials?**
A: It depends on the state and the court. Some allow digital reporting for certain case types, others do not. Verify with the board.

**Q: Can I use the same certification in federal and state court?**
A: Federal rules allow audio or steno recording, but state rules control state court work. Always confirm both.

**Q: Do I need a notary commission?**
A: Some states require it for sworn testimony sessions. Check your board rules.
